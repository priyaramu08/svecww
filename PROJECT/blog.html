The college is offering a number of courses in applied sciences field on graduate and post graduate level including Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Information Technology along with courses as Master of Business Administration. Shri Vishnu Engineering College For Women conduct courses in both undergraduate and post graduate levels, in several branches of engineering.
